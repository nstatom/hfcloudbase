Airport Ceilometer Cloud Viewer

Data Disclaimer

Hourly cloud data (larger circles) is based on hourly airport METAR readings. These observations average the past 30 minutes of ceilometer data with double weighting for the last 10 minutes of passing sky condition observations.

High rate cloud data (smaller circles) is only averaged over five minutes. The resulting ceilometer observations are more prone to overall cloud status mischaracterization in patchy cloud conditions since the observation is the result of a point measurement with little averaging.
